RFP Template for Third-Party Risk Management Software

Are you confident your third-party suppliers can deliver on their promises?

The failure of a vendor is a scary thought at any time – but it’s especially scary in these days of precarious supply chains and increased cyberattacks. If your current system isn’t providing you with the information you need to continually assess the strength of vendors, then you may need to start shopping around.

Selecting the right third-party risk management software solution can be daunting. Do you look at a TPRM point solution? Or do you look for a versatile platform that makes it easy to share risk data and collaborate across the organization? Either way, an RFP is critical to finding the right partner.

Download this template for a list of the most critical TPRM-related questions to help guide your purchase process. The questions are presented in a downloadable spreadsheet, which can be easily modified to suit your own needs.
