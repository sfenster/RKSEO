The Costs and Benefits of GRC Software

The hardest part about buying GRC software is often quantifying the ROI to justify the investment to leadership.

Forrester Consulting's study, The Total Economic ImpactTM of Riskonnect GRC, offers an in-depth cost/benefit analysis at one financial services firm in the U.S. that implemented Riskonnect's GRC software.   • Streamlining third-party risk management workflows
  • Avoiding regulatory penalties

The study also offers insight into benefits that aren't as easily quantified like the advantages of managing risk in real time, more proactively, and with better visibility across buisness units.
